Are we any different?

If you're a student, you're aware of a few popular studying tools out there. While we applaud many of the current efforts to improve the study habits and capabilities of students and all individuals, we know they're not enough.

Studying is a difficult activity to faciliate effectively for a given individual. First, what an individual should learn needs to be defined. After this, it can be figured what information this individual needs. Most importantly (and most difficultly), students should be provided with effective ways to partition concepts, filter out "noise" (unnecessary garbage), avoid redundancy, and find a happy place to consume the most amount of information--and to consume it deeply!

This is where we're here to help. Follow our progress to learn more.
